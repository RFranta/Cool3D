Welcome to Bld3r

Bld3r is a community for 3D printing tinkerers and enthusiasts.
You might love sharing your creations with others or you might simply want to print cool, quality designs.
In any case, everyone here wants the best objects.

Print freely and responsibly.




How It Works



Submit a 3D Print File

How would you like to share?


Upload


Uploading an .STL file to add your object to Bld3r.

Link


Link to an object submitted elsewhere that is freely available for download.

Torrent


Link to a torrent for files of larger 3D models.


Share freely and responsibly


Bld3r is for sharing, not for selling. All models must be free. You must be the rights holder of print uploads or respect the creator's rights
File uploads cannot be over 5mb. Link to larger files instead. Weapons and dangerous prints are prohibited.

Vote, Rate, and Flag

Be an active member of the community.


▲

Vote


Vote for objects you like. Let the community know what’s awesome.


Popularity


The most popular objects will appear on the front page.

Search results will display the most popular objects first.

Designers will earn rep points, feel happy, and keep creating great designs.


★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Rate


Rate the quality of objects.
Not all designs are equal.


Quality


Ratings help the community know which designs work.

Printing can take forever. Save others the frustration of wasting time on bad prints.

You can rate after you've printed an object and placed it on your Printshelf.

⚑

Flag


Report objects that violate terms of service.


Integrity


Flagging preserves the integrity of the community.

Objects with misleading titles and pictures will be taken down.

Weapons and dangerous objects are prohibited.






Comment and Tag

Improve objects with comments and tag edits.


Comment


Tell others what is good and bad about an object.
The top-voted comments rise to the top.


Tag

Edits


Well-defined tags make it easier for you and others to find relevant objects.





Create Tag Communities

Give objects social value.



At Bld3r, tags aren't just labels, they're communities. Tag communities define tags through an open, editable wiki.
The first 140 characters are short summaries for the search pages. Communities are free to define everything else.





Learn and Teach

3D printing can be tricky. Help each other.


Learn


Go to Bld3r University for 3D printing lessons.
Questions about 3D printing? Ask Bld3r.


Teach


Give the 3D printing community lessons.
Share a lesson or answer questions.





Earn Reputation

Gain influence.



Bld3r values active members of the community. Earn reputation when users rate your objects or when others vote on your objects or comments. Reputation unlocks special privileges such as the downvote and the powervote.
